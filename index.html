With CrossyBot's 3 Step Process:

New Trades Opened

You or CrossyBot opens a new spot, long or short trade on a USD pair.

CrossyBot Monitors PnLs

CrossyBot automatically monitors for new account balances & their EMA crosses.

CrossyBot Takes Action

Trades are closed on profitable EMA crosses against your trade direction (including all fees) or when your stop loss % has been hit.
